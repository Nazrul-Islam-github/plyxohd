Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • App usage data: features used, playback events, app open/close events
  • Crash logs and diagnostics: stack traces, error reports, ANR logs
  • Advertising identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID) or Android ID
  • Network information: connection type (Wi-Fi, cellular), general IP-based location

1.2 Information from Third-Party SDKs

We integrate the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

Crashlytics Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Collects crash reports, device state at time of crash, OS version, and device model
  • Data is used solely for app stability analysis and bug fixing
  • Does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you explicitly log custom keys
  • Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
AdMob Google AdMob
  • Displays personalized or non-personalized advertisements within the app
  • Collects advertising ID, device information, IP address, and interaction data with ads
  • May use cookies and similar technologies to serve interest-based ads
  • AdMob may share data with Google's advertising partners
  • Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

1.3 Information You Provide

PlyxoHD does not require account registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment information directly.

03

Advertising & Google AdMob

PlyxoHD is provided free of charge and supported by advertising delivered through Google AdMob. AdMob may serve personalized ads based on your interests, or non-personalized ads if you have opted out.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Going to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Resetting your advertising ID via Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
Note Opting out does not mean you will no longer see ads. It means the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

App Permissions

PlyxoHD requests the following Android permissions to deliver its core functionality. We only request permissions that are strictly necessary for the features described below.

📳
VIBRATE
Provides haptic feedback during user interactions such as gesture controls and UI confirmations inside the player. Required
⚙️
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Allows the app to run a foreground service so media playback can continue with a persistent notification visible to you. Required
▶️
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK
Declares the foreground service type as media playback, enabling proper system integration and media controls on Android 14+. Required
🌐
INTERNET
Required to load advertisements via Google AdMob and to transmit crash reports to Firebase Crashlytics. Required
🔊
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
Enables volume control via swipe gestures and proper audio focus management so the app plays and pauses in harmony with other apps. Required
🎬
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO
Allows the app to read video files stored on your device for local playback. Required on Android 13 (API 33) and above. Media Access
🖼️
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
Allows reading image files used for video thumbnail generation and folder artwork display. Required on Android 13+. Media Access
📂
READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED
Grants access only to specific media files you select via the photo picker — a privacy-preserving alternative introduced in Android 14+. Optional
📁
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Required on Android 12 (API 32) and below to browse and play video files from external storage. Not requested on Android 13+. Legacy · max API 32
💾
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Used on Android 12 and below for saving subtitle files or playback preferences to external storage. Not requested on Android 13+. Legacy · max API 32
Your Control You can review and revoke any permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → PlyxoHD → Permissions. Revoking a permission may limit certain app functionality.

What We Do NOT Access

  • We do not access your contacts, call logs, or SMS messages
  • We do not access your camera or microphone
  • We do not access your precise GPS or fine location
  • We do not access your accounts, passwords, or financial data
  • We do not upload your media files to any server — all playback is strictly local

CCPA / CPRA — Rights for California Residents

CCPA · CPRA If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provides you with specific privacy rights described in this section.

7.1 Categories of Personal Information We Collect

In the past 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA:

  • Identifiers: Advertising ID, device ID, IP address
  • Internet or Other Electronic Network Activity: App usage data, crash reports, ad interaction data
  • Geolocation Data: Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Inferences: Drawn from usage data to create a profile about app preferences

7.2 Purposes for Collection

  • Debugging, error correction, and app performance improvement
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Auditing interactions with our service
  • Performing internal research and analytics

7.3 Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

Under the CCPA/CPRA, "selling" includes sharing data for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may share your advertising ID and usage data with Google AdMob for targeted advertising, which may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" under California law.

You have the right to opt out. To do so:

  • Visit our in-app privacy settings and select "Opt Out of Ad Personalization"
  • Contact us at plyxohd@gmail.com with subject line "California Opt-Out Request"
  • Use the Android Ads settings as described in Section 3

7.4 Your CCPA/CPRA Rights

  • Know: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold
  • Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: Opt out of sale or sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Limit Use of Sensitive PI: Request we limit use of sensitive personal information to only what is necessary
  • Non-Discrimination: You will not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights

7.5 Submitting a Request

Contact us at plyxohd@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days. We may extend by an additional 45 days when reasonably necessary, with prior notice.

7.6 Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. We may require proof of authorization before processing such a request.
