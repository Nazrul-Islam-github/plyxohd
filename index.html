✦ Touch Controls

Gestures That
Just Work

PlyxoHD maps all essential controls to natural touch gestures so you never have to hunt for a button.

  • 👈
    Double-Tap Left
    Rewind 10 seconds. Tap repeatedly to keep rewinding. Duration is customizable from 5–30 seconds.
  • 👉
    Double-Tap Right
    Skip forward 10 seconds. Perfect for skipping intros and ads without lifting your eyes from the screen.
  • ↔️
    Horizontal Swipe
    Drag left or right anywhere to seek. The further from center, the faster the seek speed.
  • ☝️
    Vertical Swipe Left / Right
    Left side controls screen brightness. Right side controls media volume. Smooth, instant, accurate.
  • 🤏
    Pinch to Zoom
    Pinch open to zoom into the video. Pinch closed to zoom back out. Pan while zoomed to see any part of the frame.
  • ✊
    Long Press
    Long press screen to activate 2× speed mode — release to return to normal. Or zoom into a region by long pressing and dragging.
